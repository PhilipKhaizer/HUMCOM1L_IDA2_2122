The Witcher

Andrzej Sapkowski

The Witcher is a series of six fantasy novels and 15 short stories written by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski. The series revolves aroun the eponymous "witcher," Geralt of Rivia. "Witchers" are beast hunters who develop supernatural abilities at a young age to battle wild beasts and monsters.
The saga focuses on Geralt of Rivia and Ciri, who are linked by destiny. Ciri,princess of a recently counquered country and a pawn of international politics, becomes a witcher-in-training. Geralt is drawn into a whirlwind of events in his attemps to protect her.
